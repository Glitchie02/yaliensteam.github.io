Welcome to the (temporary) YALiEns Website!

We are a private team representing the Y.A.L.E. School of Cherry Hill. We are currently working on a new website, so expect a lot of changes in the future. To see what it looks like so far, visit the beta version.

9/20/17 - First (Official) Meeting of the Year

On September 20th 2017, we officially hosted our first robotics meeting at our school. Our team captain discussed the various parts of our robotics and FIRST Tech challenge to all the new students that have joined the team.

8/23/2017 - “4-Day Robo-Camp at Y.A.L.E. School”

Starting on August 23rd, the YALiEns Robotics team participated in a fun, educational camp taking place at Y.A.L.E School’s Ellisburg Campus. There, they learned many different things including how to code.

8/5/17 - “1-Day Workshop at MFS”

At this event, members of our team took part in a 1-Day educational event where they explored their interests in the robotics field. The team members each took a track that best suited their interests.
